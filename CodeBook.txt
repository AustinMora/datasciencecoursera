Mean 
________________________
tBodyAcc-mean()-X: Accelerometer signal for body acceleration X axial          
tBodyAcc-mean()-Y: Accelerometer signal for body acceleration Y axial          
tBodyAcc-mean()-Z: Accelerometer signal for body acceleration Z axial 

tGravityAcc-mean()-X: Accel. signal for gravity acceleration X axial      
tGravityAcc-mean()-Y: Accel. signal for gravity acceleration Y axial        
tGravityAcc-mean()-Z: Accel. signal for gravity acceleration Z axial 

tBodyAccJerk-mean()-X: Accel. signal for body jerk acceleration X axial        
tBodyAccJerk-mean()-Y: Accel. signal for body jerk acceleration Y axial      
tBodyAccJerk-mean()-Z: Accel. signal for body jerk acceleration Z axial

tBodyGyro-mean()-X: Gyroscope signal for body acceleration X axial         
tBodyGyro-mean()-Y: Gyroscope signal for body acceleration Y axial          
tBodyGyro-mean()-Z: Gyroscope signal for body acceleration Z axial         

tBodyGyroJerk-mean()-X: Gyroscope signal for body jerk acceleration X axial
tBodyGyroJerk-mean()-Y: Gyroscope signal for body jerk acceleration Y axial     
tBodyGyroJerk-mean()-Z: Gyroscope signal for body jerk acceleration Z axial

tBodyAccMag-mean(): Accel. signal for body acceleration magnitude   
tGravityAccMag-mean(): Accel. signal for grav. acceleration magn.        
tBodyAccJerkMag-mean(): Accel. signal for body jerk acceleration magn
tBodyGyroMag-mean(): Gyro. signal for body acceleration magnitude
tBodyGyroJerkMag-mean(): Gyro. signal for body jerk acceleration magnitude
